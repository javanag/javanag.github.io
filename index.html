About

Hello, I'm Javan, a student at the University of Toronto about to enter my third year. I first got into programming as a kid by playing Minecraft and wanting to make a game as cool as that. My first project was a console text adventure game. As I learned more, I began to realize that so much more could be created than games, the creative possibilities and practical applications were endless, and there will always be new problems to solve.
